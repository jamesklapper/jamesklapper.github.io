WELCOME TO THE

AUDIOLOGY CENTER OF MARYLAND

At the Audiology Center of Maryland, we provide outstanding patient care for pediatrics through geriatrics individuals who have hearing, balance, or other related problems. We provide many services, including but not limited to hearing evaluation, hearing amplification through hearing aids and assistive listening devices, tinnitus management, cerumen management (ear wax removal), and the canalith repositioning procedure for BPPV (Benign Paroxysmal Positional Vertigo) treatment.

We select our hearing aids and assistive listening devices only from the best manufactures who make and develop the most advanced technologies with a wide array of models to choose from, as we believe one size does not fit all.

WHY CHOOSE THE

AUDIOLOGY CENTER OF MARYLAND?

At the Audiology Center of Maryland, we utilize cutting-edge equipment with high experience and care. Dr. Maryam Esmaeli is strongly committed to continuing education in the Audiology field in order to ensure that we provide you with the best care currently possible. As an Audiologist, Dr. Esmaeli is the specialist and has the highest training and experience to cater the specific needs of patients suffering from hearing-related difficulties.

Under the care of Dr. Esmaeli, you can rest assured that your specific hearing solutions will be designed around your individual hearing needs.
